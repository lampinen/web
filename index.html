Bio:

I am a PhD student in the Department of Psychology at Stanford University. I am in the cognitive area, and am advised by Professor James McClelland. My background is in mathematics, physics, and machine learning. I am interested in mathematical cognition, specifically higher-level mathematical abilities such as abstraction and proof, and how the relationships between concepts and the presentation of concepts affect learning. I am also interested in AI and machine learning.
